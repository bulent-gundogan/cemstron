CMG-400 NDIR

Insitu NDIR Gas Analyzer

Cemstron offers CMG-400 insitu stack gas analyzer. The CMG-400 is a multi-channel flue gas analyser that uses the principle of Non-Dispersive Infrared Absorption (NDIR) technology. It provides high accuracy, low maintenance measurement of all the key combustion process emissions gases, up to four (4) on each cconfiguration. It is suitable operating in very high dust loading conditions, up to 5 gm/m3 of dust.

The CMG-400 can be installed on a variety of processes and applications, including combustion turbines, coal fired boiler economizer outlets
